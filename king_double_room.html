King Double Room, £110

The King double room is a first floor room overlooking the walled garden at the rear of the house. Decorated in a sumptuous turquoise, black and gold colour scheme this exotic room has a touch of the Orient and days of the Grand Tour about it. Alongside the Flat Screen TV and black marble side tables are interesting objet d'art from the owners travels in the Far East. A king sized double bed made up with down duvets and a choice of feather or foam pillows ensures a very comfortable nights sleep. The luxury en-suite bathroom has a very large walk in shower. There is a well stocked beverage tray with kettle, coffee and tea making facilities and bottled Harrogate Spa water. Fine cotton bathrobes with matching slippers are provided for guests use.
