Ground Floor Twin Room, £110

Ground Floor Twin room overlooks the beautiful walled garden at the rear of the house. The small dining room is opposite and on the same level so it is particularly suitable for any guests who prefer to avoid the stairs. Decorated in a very pretty peony theme, this bright sunny room lifts the spirits. The twin beds are larger sized and made up with down duvets and a choice of feather or foam pillows ensuring a very comfortable nights sleep. The en-suite bathroom has a large walk in shower with grip handles. There is a seeting area with two armchairs a coffee table and a flat screen TV. A well stocked beverage tray with kettle, coffee and tea making facilities and bottled Harrogate Spa water is provided. There are fine cotton bathrobes with matching slippers in the wardrobes for guests use.
