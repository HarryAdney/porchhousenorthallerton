Stay in splendour in the heart of Northallerton

Porch House is a historic Elizabethan town house in the centre of Northallerton, North Yorkshire.

Built in 1584 it is Northallerton's oldest private dwelling.

Three en-suite luxury guest rooms each with free Wi-Fi, flat screen Tv's with free view, complimentary luxury toiletries, hairdryers and fine cotton robes.

All major Debit & Credit cards are accepted, and there is ample private parking.

The Railway Station, local and national shops and traditional Yorkshire pubs and restaurants of Northallerton are all within walking distance.

Northallerton is ideally located in North Yorkshire, being within easy travelling distance of many of the loveliest places to visit in the county. From our award-winning coast to the beautiful countryside of the Yorkshire Moors and the Yorkshire Dales, there's plenty to see and do in the region.

Tourist hotspots like York, Wensleydale, Scarborough and Whitby are all close by. For the active, we are near the Coast to Coast Walk
