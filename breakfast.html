Breakfast

The traditional Yorkshire Breakfast is renowned at Porch House, beautifully served on fine china, with crystal glasses and silver cutlery. All Aga cooked, which not only gives traditional bacon and eggs more flavour it's also a much healthier option.

Choose from a buffet selection of fresh fruits, cereals, freshly squeezed orange juice and yogurt while your breakfast is cooked to order. We offer smoked Salmon from Staithes with scrambled eggs, omelettes and traditional full Yorkshire breakfast. We use locally sourced produce of the highest quality. Masham Sausage Shop for sausages, dry cured bacon and black pudding and our eggs are free range from Finghall And don't forget to try the home made marmalade and jams with organic bread.
